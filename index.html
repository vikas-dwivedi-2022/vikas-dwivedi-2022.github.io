About

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the CREATIS Biomedical Imaging Laboratory (INSA Lyon). Previously, I was a postdoc at SUNY‚ÄìAlbany (USA) and at the Robert Bosch Center for Data Science and AI, IIT Madras (India). My work lies at the intersection of domain-informed AI, physics-/biophysics-informed learning, and fast, interpretable solvers for PDE-constrained problems in fluid mechanics and medical imaging.
